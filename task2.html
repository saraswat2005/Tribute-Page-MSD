Mahendra Singh Dhoni

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, popularly known as 'Thala', is one of the greatest cricketers to ever play for India. His calm nature, sharp cricketing mind, and leadership skills have earned him a permanent place in the hearts of millions.

Career Achievements

  • World Cup Wins: 2007 (T20), 2011 (ODI)
  • Indian Premier League (IPL) Titles: 4 (with Chennai Super Kings)
  • Most Successful Indian Captain in limited-overs cricket

Personality

MS Dhoni is known for his calm and composed nature. His leadership is often praised for inspiring his team and his ability to stay cool under pressure, earning him the nickname "Captain Cool".

Retirement

  • MS Dhoni retired from international cricket on August 15, 2020.
  • He also retired from IPL, playing his last game in 2024 with Chennai Super Kings.
